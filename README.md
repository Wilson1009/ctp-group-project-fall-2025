### MAPS:

Tired of confusing course catalogs and academic planning stress?

**MAPS** is the **interactive, gamified, and data-driven** solution designed specifically for the **Queens College CS major**. 

We turn complex degree requirements into a clear, manageable path, helping you:

* **Gain Clarity:** Effortlessly understand your remaining degree requirements.
* **Make Smarter Choices:** Use professor data and reviews to pick the best sections.
* **Stay Motivated:** Engage with a gamified system that tracks your progress.
* **Plan Confidently:** Build an optimized academic schedule from day one to graduation.
